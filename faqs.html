3. Is prior cruise ship experience necessary?
• While previous cruise ship experience is preferred for some roles, it is not always required. We offer various positions suited for different levels of experience.
4. How long does the recruitment process take?
• The recruitment process typically takes between 1 to 4 months, depending on the role and the number of applicants. We will keep you updated throughout the process.
5. What are the benefits of working on a cruise ship?
• Working on a cruise ship allows you to travel the world while earning a competitive salary. Additionally, you receive free accommodation and meals, and there are opportunities for career advancement.
6. What is the duration of contracts?
• Most contracts last between 6 to 9 months, with the possibility of renewal based on performance and availability.
7. What positions are currently available?
• We have various positions available in hospitality, medical, technical departments, and entertainment. Please check our Current Job Openings for more details.
8. Will I need a visa to work on a cruise ship?
• Yes, most positions require a work visa depending on the cruise ship's location. Our team will assist you in obtaining the necessary visas before your departure.
9. Can I travel with my family while working on a cruise ship?
• Unfortunately, crew members cannot bring family members onboard. However, you may visit your family during leave periods between contracts.
10. What is the application process like?
• Our application process is designed to be straightforward and transparent. After submitting your application online, our recruitment team will review your qualifications. If you are shortlisted, we'll invite you for an initial interview, which may be conducted over the phone or via video call. Successful candidates will then proceed to further interviews or assessments relevant to the position. Throughout the process, we'll keep you updated on your application status.
