Who We Are


Travo Agency is a recruitment agency based in Morocco, helping people find jobs on cruise ships. We work with well known cruise lines and connect them with skilled workers for jobs in hospitality, cooking, and medical services. Our goal is to make it easier for Africans to start exciting careers at sea.


Requirements established by cruise lines:


  • You must be at least 21 years old.
  • You need to speak English well.
  • Most starting jobs need at least 1 year of work experience.
  • Contracts can be different depending on the cruise line.
  • All shipboard jobs require crew members to work 7 days a week.
  • A strong work ethic is essential.

Our Mission

At Travo, our mission is to connect talented individuals with cruise ship careers that allow them to grow, explore, and thrive. We believe in making the recruitment process easier and more transparent for professionals across Africa.

We partner with top cruise lines to provide exciting job opportunities in the hospitality, culinary, and technical industries, helping our clients achieve their career goals while traveling the world.
